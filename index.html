Thoughts for May 2018

My 6-year old is loving the Champ Soccer Program run out of Calvary Churchand my 4-year old adores Calvary's HUGE sand-filled playground.

I, for one, adore that said playground is not only fenced in - but has three seperate play areas that entertain my kids for hours! After soccer practice, we'll set up camp at the playground and eat lunch at the picnic tables. Crucial bonus: Calvary's concession stand serves Chic Fil A! Not only do my kids have fun, but there's no lunch mess at the house to clean, and they're all tuckered out by the time we do get home.
